<p><strong><u>Bioenergy&nbsp;</u></strong>and&nbsp;<strong><u>energy from waste&nbsp;</u></strong>accounts for&nbsp;<strong>8.1%</strong>&nbsp;of all renewable electricity generated in Scotland in&nbsp;<strong>2018</strong>&nbsp;(<strong>2,134 GWh</strong>, which is the equivalent of powering all households in City of Edinburgh and Glasgow City local authorities for a year).&nbsp; There is&nbsp;<strong>385 MW&nbsp;</strong>of bioenergy projects in the&nbsp;<strong>pipeline</strong>,&nbsp;<strong>205 MW&nbsp;</strong>coming from biomass,&nbsp;<strong>172 MW&nbsp;</strong>from energy from waste.</p>
