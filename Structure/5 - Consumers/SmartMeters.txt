<p>The UK Government required supply companies to offer <strong>smart meters </strong>to their customers to enable them to monitor their energy usage. Data from Electralink suggests that as of <strong>January 2020</strong>, almost <strong>1.07 million </strong>smart meters were installed in Scotland, representing <strong>38.2%</strong> of all meters in Scotland. This is up from <strong>9.7%</strong> in <strong>November 2016</strong>.</p>
<p>Greater progress has been made in the smart meter rollout in South Scotland compared to North Scotland &ndash; <strong>42.5%</strong> of all <strong>South Scotland</strong> meters have been installed compared to <strong>27.1%</strong> in <strong>North Scotland</strong>.</p>
