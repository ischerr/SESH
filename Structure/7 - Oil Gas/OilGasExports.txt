<p>In <strong>2018</strong>, <strong>82.3% </strong>of all primary <strong>oil </strong>and <strong>gas </strong>energy in Scotland was <strong>exported</strong>.</p>
<p>Turnover of Scottish offshore oil and gas exports stands at <strong>&pound;17.4 billion </strong>in turnover in <strong>2017</strong>, of which <strong>&pound;10.6 billion </strong>is exported to the <strong>rest of the UK </strong>and <strong>&pound;6.8 billion</strong> exported to the <strong>rest of the world</strong>. The value of exports <strong>increased </strong>by <strong>&pound;4 billion </strong>from <strong>2016</strong>. 2017 is the first year the value of exports increased since 2012, likely to be a consequence of <strong>production stabilising </strong>and <strong>prices rising</strong>.</p>
