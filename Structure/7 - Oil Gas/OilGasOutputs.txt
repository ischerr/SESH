<p>Of all of Scotland&rsquo;s primary energy generated via oil and gas, more than four fifths (<strong>82.3%</strong>) of it was <strong>exported</strong> in <strong>2018</strong>, with only about a tenth (<strong>10.7%</strong>) domestically <strong>consumed</strong>.
