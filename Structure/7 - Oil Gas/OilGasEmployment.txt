<p>The oil and gas sector still has an important role to play in Scotland in terms of employment. In <strong>2018</strong>, the <strong>offshore oil </strong>and <strong>gas </strong>industry supports an estimated <strong>101,400 jobs</strong> in <strong>Scotland</strong>, <strong>similar to 2017</strong>. It still is the main sector in Scotland for employment relating to energy, and makes up <strong>39%</strong> of all oil and gas employment in the <strong>UK</strong>.</p>
