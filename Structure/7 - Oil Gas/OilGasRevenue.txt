<p>Estimated<strong> sales </strong>of oil and gas produced in Scotland are <strong>&pound;25.0 billion </strong>in the <strong>2018/19 </strong>financial year. Sales income <strong>rose</strong> for the third year in a row, up from <strong>&pound;19.5 billion</strong> on the previous financial year. The increased sales revenue is mostly due to the recovery in oil prices.</p>
